About Us

In the quiet town of Ossu, nestled within the heart of Ghana, there's a restaurant that has steadily grown into something more than a place to eat. It's a landmark, a symbol, and a gathering ground.

It was during one summer in Istanbul, dining along the actual Bosphorus strait, that Selim had a moment of clarity. As he watched ferries glide between continents and families share meals under glowing lanterns, he realized food had the power to transcend borders.
